The strength of agree/disagree on a cline:

| <----------------------------------------------------> |
^                          ^                             ^ 
|                          |                             |
Strongly                 Neutral                  Strongly
disagree                                             agree

Strong agreement:
     ,
{Absolutely
Totally
Fully
Completely} + agree

I agree entirely with your point on this.
I'm in total/complete agreement/accord.
I'm of exactly the same opinion.

Neutral agreement

I agree.
I think we are in agreement on that.
I can accept your position on that (sounds a little reluctant).

Partial agreement:

I would tent to agree with you on that.
I agree with you on the whole, but...
By and large, I would accept your views but...

Strongly disagreement:

I totally/compleltely/wholly disagree with you.
What are you saying/suggesting is just not feasible.

Softening Strong Disagreement:

Frankly...
To be quite frank,...
To put it bluntly,...
With respect,...
With due respect,...

I reckon that/ I see your point + I have some doubt about it/I'm afraid I cannot agree with you.

Neutral disagreement:

I really can't agree with you on that.
I can't say that I share your view.
I'm not totally convinced by tyour argument.
I can't help feeling that...
I really must take issue with you here(argue).

Softening Neutrla disagreement:
I'm afraid ... 
I'm sorry...
I respect your opinion, of course, however ...

Tactful Disagreement:
To a certain extent I agre with you, but...
I take.see your point, but have you considered..?
