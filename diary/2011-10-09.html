2011-10-09

又连续写了好几天的代码了，我在想下面这种情况：

The human beings are constructing a race-supporting software system, which runs for thousands of years. In the future, nobody knows how this ancient system was built,nor do people maintain it. Top coders around the world gathered facing the task. Once mistaken, thousands of lives wasted. Once crashed, human race annihilated.

然后又回到了现实，发现这种宏大设想简直就是内心抱怨的直接投影。
